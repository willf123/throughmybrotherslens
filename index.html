The story behind this

A daily gift

My brother has always seen the world differently — he notices the light before anyone else does. A few years ago he started sending me a photo every morning. Not to show off, not to caption, just to share. This gallery is my way of giving those pictures a home, and of sharing that small daily beauty with anyone who wanders by.
